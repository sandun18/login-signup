*testDigital Signals
• Most of digital signals are aperiodic and thus, period or frequency is
not appropriate.
• Bit Interval (Instead of period)
• The bit interval is the time required to send one single bit.
• Bit rate (Instead of frequency)
• Number of bit intervals per second. So, bit rate is the number of bits sent
in one second.
• Expressed in bits per second (bps)*Test
